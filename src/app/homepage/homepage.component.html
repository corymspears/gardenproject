Welcome



Let's make a nice big leafy tree. You could sit here for weeks with your one hair brush trying to do that - or you could do it with one stroke with an almighty brush. Van Dyke Brown is a very nice brown, it's almost like a chocolate brown. These things happen automatically. All you have to do is just let them happen. We start with a vision in our heart, and we put it on canvas. Talk to trees, look at the birds. Whatever it takes. Be brave. All you need to paint is a few tools, a little instruction, and a vision in your mind. When you do it your way you can go anywhere you choose.
